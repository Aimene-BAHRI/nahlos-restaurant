Our Story

Crafted with passion,
served with heart

Nahlo's was born from a deep love for Spanish cuisine and the vibrant culinary traditions of the Mediterranean. Every dish is a journey — from the saffron-infused paella negra to our fresh tapas made with locally sourced ingredients.

Our host Ihab and the entire team pour genuine warmth into every visit, making sure each guest feels like they've stepped into a little corner of Spain right here in Oran.

— Ihab & the Nahlo's Team
